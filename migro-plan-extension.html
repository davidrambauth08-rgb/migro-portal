🔐 The Bridging Visa Safety Net

If you lodge a new visa application BEFORE your current visa expires, a Bridging Visa A (BVA) is automatically issued. This lets you stay lawfully in Australia while your application is processed. The BVA carries the same work and study rights as your current visa. The key rule: lodge before your visa expires — even 1 day matters.

🚨
Do NOT overstay your visa
If your visa expires before you lodge a new application, you become unlawfully present in Australia. This triggers automatic exclusion periods (3 years for overstaying under 28 days, potentially a lifetime ban for longer) and will likely invalidate future applications.

Check ImmiAccount at least once a week and ensure your email is current. The Department sends RFI notices through ImmiAccount with a typical 28-day response deadline. Missing it is one of the most common reasons for visa refusal.

Pro tip: Enable email notifications in ImmiAccount settings, and add your migration agent as a secondary contact for simultaneous RFI alerts.
