Regional study bonus

If your principal place of study was in a regional area of Australia (outside major cities), you may be entitled to an additional 1 year on your 485. Regional areas include most locations outside Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, and the Gold Coast.

Quick eligibility check

How to apply

1
Confirm your course completion date
Your course completion date is the date all requirements were met (last exam, final submission) — not your graduation ceremony. You have 6 months from this date to lodge. Request your official course completion letter from your institution.
2
Gather documents
You'll need: passport, course completion letter from institution, academic transcripts, English test results (if required), health insurance details (OSHC or equivalent), and skills assessment (if Graduate Work stream).
3
Lodge on ImmiAccount
Create or log in to your ImmiAccount and select 'Apply for a visa'. Choose subclass 485. Complete all forms and upload documents. Pay the visa application charge ($1,895 for primary applicant).
4
Complete health examination
Book a health examination with an approved panel physician. You'll receive an HAP (Health Assessment Portal) ID after lodging your visa. Health exams cost $300–$500 per person.
5
Await decision
Processing is typically 2–6 months. You'll receive a Bridging Visa A while waiting if your student visa is about to expire. Once granted, you can travel in and out of Australia freely.

What to do on your 485

📋 Build your points score
Every year of skilled employment in Australia adds 5 points (up to 10 points for regional). Use your 485 time to accumulate experience, improve English, and hit invitation-worthy scores.

English test — are you exempt?

You may be exempt from the English test requirement if you hold a passport from the UK, USA, Canada, New Zealand, or Ireland. You may also be exempt if you completed at least 5 years of study (primary or secondary) in English in one of these countries. Otherwise, you'll need IELTS 6.0 in each band, PTE 50 in each component, or equivalent.

Strategy tips

⏰
Apply as soon as possibleThe 6-month window to apply runs from course completion, not graduation. Apply early to get your Bridging Visa and stay lawful if your student visa is expiring.
📍
Study in a regional area for the extra yearIf you're choosing where to study, picking a regional institution can give you an extra year on your 485 AND regional study bonus points for the points test.
📋
Get the right streamGraduate Work stream gives only 18 months but doesn't require 2 years of study. Post-Study Work is longer but requires at least a Bachelor degree and 2 years study in Australia.
💼
Get skilled work in your fieldAustralian work experience is worth 5–10 points in the points test. Even 1 year of skilled work post-485 significantly boosts your EOI competitiveness.
