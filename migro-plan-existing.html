⚡ Key Advantage of Being Onshore

Because you are already in Australia, you can lodge most visa applications without leaving the country. Once you lodge, a Bridging Visa A (BVA) is automatically granted, letting you remain in Australia legally while your application is processed — even if your current visa expires in the meantime.

⚠️
Check your visa expiry date
You must lodge your new application BEFORE your current visa expires to be eligible for a Bridging Visa. If your visa has already expired, you may be unlawfully present. Check your visa status on VEVO →

Confirm 485 eligibility (graduates only)
485 pathway
▼

For the 485 Graduate visa, you must have completed an eligible qualification at an Australian educational institution while holding a student visa. The degree must have taken at least 2 academic years to complete. You must also have held a student visa for at least some of the time you were studying.

Eligibility requirements
🎓Completed a bachelor's, master's, or doctoral degree from an Australian institution
📅Applied within 6 months of completing your degree (6 months grace period)
✅Satisfy the English language requirement (IELTS 6.0 overall)
🏥Meet health and character requirements
Visa Length

Unlimited

Tip: If you studied a STEM degree in a regional area, you may qualify for up to 5 years on your 485 visa instead of 2. Check the regional study requirement.
Confirm 482 eligibility (employer sponsor)
482 pathway
▼

The 482 TSS visa requires an approved sponsor employer, a nominated occupation on the relevant list (MLTSSL or STSOL), and the required skills/qualifications. The employer must pay at least the market salary rate and the Temporary Skilled Migration Income Threshold (TSMIT), currently AUD $70,000/year.

Key requirements
🏢Employer must be an approved standard business sponsor (SBS)
📋Occupation on the Skills in Demand (SID) list or MLTSSL
💰Salary at or above TSMIT (AUD $70,000/year)
🗣️English: IELTS 5.0 average each band (minimum)
Visa Length
Up to 4 years
Cost (you)
AUD $3,115

Book health examination with approved panel physician
Mandatory
▼

All 482 and 485 applicants must undergo a medical examination conducted by an approved panel physician in Australia. The exam includes a chest X-ray, blood tests, and a general physical assessment. You'll receive a HAP ID linked to your ImmiAccount.

What to bring
🛂Valid passport (original)
🔢HAP ID from ImmiAccount
📸2 passport-size photos
📋Medical records if you have a pre-existing condition

12 months

Respond to any requests for further information (RFI)
28-day deadline
▼

During processing, the Department may send you a Request for Further Information via ImmiAccount. You typically have 28 days to respond. Failing to respond in time can result in your application being refused. Check your ImmiAccount and email regularly.

📄Updated passport biodata page (if renewed)
📄Statutory declaration for name changes or identity

Start planning your pathway to Permanent Residency
Next milestone
▼

Now that you have a pathway visa, the next goal is Permanent Residency. From a 485, you'd typically seek employer sponsorship (482) then 186 ENS after 3 years. From a 482, you may be eligible for 186 ENS Transition Stream after 3 years with the same employer.

PR pathways from here
🏆Subclass 186 ENS (Transition) — 3 years on 482 with same employer
⭐Subclass 189 — Independent skilled points-tested stream
📍Subclass 190 — State-nominated points-tested (5 bonus points)
