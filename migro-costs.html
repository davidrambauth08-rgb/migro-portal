When do you pay each cost?

Migration costs are spread across 3 distinct phases. Budget for each phase separately.

Phase 1 — Before EOI
Preparation costs
These are paid upfront, before you submit your Expression of Interest.
Skills assessment English test Translations (NAATI) Document certification
Typical range: $700 – $2,500
Phase 2 — After EOI, before ITA
State nomination costs (190/491 only)
If applying for state nomination, there may be a small application fee.
State nomination fee ($0–$300)
Typical range: $0 – $300
Phase 3 — After ITA (60-day window)
The big payment — visa application
The largest costs arrive once you receive your ITA. You have 60 days to pay and lodge your complete application.
Visa Application Charge Health examinations Police clearances Migration agent
Typical range: $5,000 – $12,000+ (depends on family size)
Visa granted 🎉
No more migration fees
Once granted, no further fees to the Department. Settlement costs (flights, housing, etc.) are separate.

Save money on your migration

Smart strategies to reduce costs without compromising your application.

1
Get your documents right the first time
A negative skills assessment means paying again to reapply ($200–$750+). Invest time getting reference letters and documents right before submitting — far cheaper than repeating.
2
Use paper IELTS if you're near-proficient
Paper IELTS (~$295) is significantly cheaper than computer-based (~$365) or PTE (~$470). Same result for skilled migration. Some score better on computer — try a practice test first to decide.
3
DIY your EOI — agents aren't needed for this step
Submitting an EOI is free and you don't need an agent for this step. If you hire an agent, focus them on the full visa application (after ITA) where mistakes are most costly — not the EOI itself.
4
Save before you get invited — not after
You have 60 days from invitation to pay the VAC and lodge. For a couple with 2 kids, the VAC alone exceeds $9,000. Have this money ready before the invitation arrives — not after.
5
Request a group discount for police clearances
If you and your partner need clearances from the same country, request simultaneously — some authorities offer discounts. Also, start early: Brazil, India, Philippines can take 4–8 weeks to process.
