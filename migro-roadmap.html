1 day to submit
Apply with all required documents. Pay the assessment fee ($400–$1,000). Processing takes 4–24 weeks — start this as early as possible.

🗣️ Take your English test
Prepare 1–3 months
Minimum: Competent (IELTS 6.0 each band). For +10 pts: Proficient (7.0). For +20 pts: Superior (8.0). Results valid 3 years. Higher English is often the fastest points booster.
IELTS tracker & tips

✅ Receive positive skills assessment
4–24 weeks after submission
If positive, you're ready to submit an EOI. If negative, you can request a review or reapply with stronger evidence. Many successful applicants needed a second attempt.

💻 Create ImmiAccount & submit EOI
1–2 days
Submit your EOI via ImmiAccount. Select all visa subclasses and states you're eligible for. Submit as early as possible — date in pool is the tie-breaker when scores are equal.
EOI guide
○
🗺️ Apply for state nomination 190 491
4–12 weeks
Apply to state government immigration programs. Apply to multiple states simultaneously. A job offer or current residence in the state significantly improves chances.
State nomination guide
○
⏳ Wait in SkillSelect pool
Weeks to years
Rounds run ~twice per month, highest points first then EOI date. Use this time to accumulate Australian work experience, improve English, or update your EOI if your score changes.

✉️ Receive Invitation to Apply (ITA)
60-day deadline starts now
Notification via ImmiAccount. You have exactly 60 days to lodge — no extensions. Have your documents and funds ready before this moment arrives.

4
Phase 4 — Visa application (60-day window)
Within 60 days
○
📂 Gather all application documents
Week 1–3
Collect all evidence: employment, skills assessment, English results, identity, police clearances (all countries 12+ months), and relationship evidence if applicable.
Full document checklist
○
🏥 Complete health examinations
Week 2–4
Book with a HAP-approved panel physician. Typically includes chest X-ray and general medical. Results link electronically to your case. Book early as slots fill up.
○
🔎 Obtain police clearance certificates
2–8 weeks
Required from every country where you've lived 12+ months since age 16. Australia: days. India/Brazil/Philippines: 4–8 weeks. Request these early as they often cause delays.
○
💳 Lodge application & pay VAC
Day 45–55 (before day 60!)
Upload all documents and pay the non-refundable VAC. Do this before day 55 to leave buffer. Double-check all documents are attached before clicking submit.
VAC fee table
5
Phase 5 — Processing & grant
6–18+ months
○
📋 Application under assessment
6–18 months typically
A case officer will be assigned. Keep ImmiAccount monitored — respond to any s56 (further information request) within 28 days. Ensure passport and English results don't expire during processing.
○
🎉 Visa granted!
The finish line
Notification via email and ImmiAccount. Visa links electronically to your passport — no sticker. For 190/491: first entry must be via the nominating state. For 491: live and work in a regional area.
○
🌿 Apply for subclass 191 PR 491 only
After 3 years on 491
After 3 years living and working in a regional area on the 491, you become eligible for the subclass 191 — Permanent Residence (Regional). This grants unconditional PR.
